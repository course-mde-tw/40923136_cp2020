while fiber-optic cable is great for its speed，distance and security，it hasn`t yet become commonplace in most networks.instead，we are still using the same type of cabling we have using for the past two to three decades--twsited pair.Twsited pair cable is most popular physical LAN media in the world today.Each cable contains four pairs of individually insulated strands of copper wire.Each pair is twsited together to minimize the amount of electromagnetic interference(EMI).The more twsite in the cable ，the less EMI there will be.Twsited Pair cable is broken up into two type:unshielded twsited pair (UTP) and shielded twsited pair.

雖然光纖電纜的速度、距離和安全性能非常優異，但是它仍然在許多電腦工作的地方並不普及。儘管如此，我們仍舊用某些類型的電纜，將它放在一些電纜壽命需要超過二十年至三十年的地方-雙絞線。雙絞線是現在世界上最火紅的物理性LAN介質.每一條電纜包含了四對獨立絕緣的銅線。每一對絞合在一起，用最大能力減緩電磁波的干擾。電纜線有越多雙絞線包含在裡面，電磁波就越難發生。雙絞線有兩種類型:非屏蔽式和屏蔽式。

Shielded twsited pair cable is built very similarly to unshielded twsited pair，except that the pair of wires are also surrouded by a metallic shielded (like foil).This outer foil shielded minimize

the EMI to which the cable exposed.This increases the security and reliability of the cable .Unfortunately，however，this also increases the cost of the cable，so it is typecally used only in high-production envirements where the extra security is worth the extra cost.

遮蔽式雙絞線和非遮蔽式雙絞線的構造非常相似，那它們有甚麼地方不一樣呢?它們不同之處在於屏蔽雙絞線中成對的電線也被金屬屏蔽層(例如說金屬箔)包覆，外部箔屏蔽層可大幅減輕電纜因暴露在電磁波的干擾中所受的傷害，這個方法大幅增加了電纜的安全性和可靠度，但很不幸的是，這個方法會增加額外的成本，所以在需要多出來的成本產生額外的高安全性的那些高生產力環境比較常見。

The more common type of cabling is unshielded twsited pair cable.Instead of using extra shielded，this cable relies on the twsiting of its copper pairs to minimize the effects of EMI.Each twsited pair is twsited to insulate itself from the other pairs，and becuase there is no additional shielding，it's available at a lower cost than STP.Therefore，UTP is most common media of choice for small office,home office,residential and many commercial networks.

非遮蔽雙絞線類型比遮蔽式雙絞線類型的普及率還高，而且該類型的雙絞線不需要另外的遮蔽，它依靠銅線絞合來產生抵抗電磁波干擾的最大效能，每一個雙絞線經過絞合後將自身與其它絞線絕緣，再來因為它沒有額外的覆層，所以可以用比遮蔽行雙絞線低的價格取得它。因此非遮蔽型雙絞線對小型辦公室、家庭工廠、住宅和許多商業網路最常選擇的大眾媒體。

Twsited pair cable is categorized based on category (cat)number.For example,cat 3,category 3,is the oldest type of cable that we use.It was introducted back in the 1980s and 1990s for use in the first star topologies.The maximum throughput or bandwidth of cat 3 cablingis only 10 Mbps(megabits per second).The cat 3,like all twsited pair cabling,can't go ferther than 100 meters without using a repeater,switch,or active hub to amplify the signal.This limited distance is attributable to the copper wiring in these cables. As the signal travels over the copper wire,the netural resistance to wire decreases the signal's strength,and it eventually become too weak for the distant end to receive.For this reason,there is a 100 meter limitation when building and designing a twsited pair network.

雙絞線是以類別加數字來做分類。舉例來說，我們使用最早的電纜類型是類別3(cat 3)。這要從80年代和90年代用於第一批星型拓樸結構開始介紹起，cat 3電纜的最大吞吐量或頻寬只有10Mbps。cat 3電纜和其它雙絞線電纜一樣，使用中繼器、交換機或有源集線器來放大信號，cat 3才能超過100米。這個有限的距離歸因於在電纜中的銅線。當在銅線上傳輸信號時，銅線的自然電阻會減弱信號的強度，減弱到最後會無法接收遠端資訊。也因為如此，這100公尺的長度變成是建構或設計光纖網路時的阻礙。

Cat 5 cables were an improvement on the older cat 3 cables and contain more twists, giving them more shielding from EMI and a maximum bandwidth of 100 Mbps. Cat 5e, or cat 5 enhanced, increased the speed of the maximum throughput to one Gbps, or 1000 Mbps, and became a gap filler until cat 6 cabling became commonplace.

Cat 5電纜比Cat 3電纜更進一步擁有更多的雙絞線，這些雙絞線讓Cat 5電纜抵抗電磁波干擾時有更強的能力，而且擁有最大吞吐量來到100Mbps或1000Mbps的效能，並且填補在Cat 6電纜更普及化前的空缺。

Cat 6a overcomes the 37-55 meter limitation of cat 6 when used in 10GBaseT. Cat 7, the newest standard, is shielded and provides connections up to 10 Gbps with a maximum distance of 100 meters. “Cat 7 and cat 7a cabling will be used for backbone connections between servers within a data center” and “provide an alternative to using fiber-optic cabling within the data center” 

Cat 6ay在10GBaseT使用時的限制比Cat 6超過37~55公尺，來到137公尺~155公尺。最新的標準Cat 7"有屏蔽，且能提供最大為10Mbps的帶寬、100公尺的距離。"Cat 7和Cat 7a將用於數據中心內伺服器的主幹連接"並且"提供一個讓數據中心使用光纖電纜的選擇"。

All cable categories up to cat 5e and cat 6a have a maximum distance of 100 meters. When used in 10GBaseT, the maximum distance for cat 6 is 55 meters. Cat 7 is shielded and exceeds the 100 meter limit. As the category of the cable increases, so do the number of twists per inch and the frequency range, allowing for more data and improved protection against EMI. Best practice for copper cabling is not to run cable lengths to the maximum because attenuation will reduce data capacity.

在所有電纜線的類別中，能傳輸最大距離100公尺的電纜線只有Cat 5e和Cat 6a。當使用Cat 6傳輸10GBaseT的資料時，它最多只能傳輸55公尺。Cat 7因為擁有遮蔽的技術，所以它能超過100公尺的傳輸限制。隨著纜線類別的增加，每英吋的捻數和頻率也跟著增加以支持更多、更快的資料傳輸和更好的保護以防止受到電磁波的干擾。最好的銅線不是越長越好，而是傳輸越多資料以達到越高的效率才越好。

Twisted pair cables come with three main types of connectors. The first is the RJ-45 connector, which is an eight-pin connector used in Ethernet networks. Even though most Ethernet networks use only four pins, the RJ-45 provides eight pins for future use and capabilities. The first four pins are used to transmit (two pins) and receive (two pins).

雙絞線有三種類型的連接器。第一種是RJ-45連接器，它是用乙太網路的八針連接器，儘管大部分的乙太網路都使用四針連接器，RJ-45仍因應未來的使用和效能而提供八針連接器，前四個引針中的兩個是用來傳輸，另外兩個則是接收訊息。

The RJ-11 connector is used in the standard telephone system. The RJ-11 is a six-pin connector, but commonly only two pins are used for a telephone (transmit and receive). If you have a two-line phone, then four pins are used: two pins to transmit and two pins to receive.

RJ-11連接器通常用在標準的的電話系統，它有6針連接器，但是電話通常只會用兩針連接器(用來傳輸和接收訊息)，如果您有一個雙線電話，則另外四線連接器中的兩線用來傳輸訊號，剩下兩線用於接收訊號。

Finally, the DB-9 connector is used for RS-232 signals. This connector looks like a standard serial connector. It is used commonly for asynchronous serial communications, like connecting to an external modem. In modern networks, you will see this type of connector on a “rollover cable.” A rollover cable has a DB-9 on one side (which is connected to your laptop’s serial port), and an RJ-45 connector on the other side (which is used to connect to Cisco routers and switches to do administration tasks)

最後，DB-9連接器用於傳輸和接收RS-232信號，它看起來像標準的串聯連接器，通常用於異步串聯異步串聯通信，譬如連接至外部的調製解調器。在現代網路世界中，您將會看到這類型的連接器在"轉換電路"之上。一個轉換電纜中的一側有DB-9連接器(它是用來連接筆電的端子接口)，然後一個RJ-45連接器在其它地方(用來連接Cisco路由器和交換機以執行管理任務)

A straight-through cable is a type of twisted pair cable that is used in local area networks to connect a computer to a network hub, such as a router. This type of cable is also sometimes called a patch cable. Both ends of the cable have an identical pinout, hence the name “straight-through.” A crossover cable switches the pinouts of the two ends of the cable, making the transmit and receive pins alternate on the two ends. This allows two workstations to connect directly to each other without the need for a hub, switch, or router.

直通電纜是一種使用於區域網路連接到網路集線器的雙絞線，像是路由器。這類型的電纜有時也稱為跳線。在電纜的兩端有相同的引出線，所以稱作"直通"。交叉電纜切換電纜兩端的分配，讓兩端的引出線擁有輸出或接收的選擇，這樣的方式可令工作站不需要路由器、交換器和網路集線器的導引就可連接到其他人的電腦。
