while fiber-optic cable is great for its speed，distance and security，it hasn`t yet become commonplace in most networks.instead，we are still using the same type of cabling we have using for the past two to three decades--twsited pair.Twsited pair cable is most popular physical LAN media in the world today.Each cable contains four pairs of individually insulated strands of copper wire.Each pair is twsited together to minimize the amount of electromagnetic interference(EMI).The more twsite in the cable ，the less EMI there will be.Twsited Pair cable is broken up into two type:unshielded twsited pair (UTP) and shielded twsited pair.

雖然光纖電纜的速度、距離和安全性能非常優異，但是它仍然在許多電腦工作的地方並不普及。儘管如此，我們仍舊用某些類型的電纜，將它放在一些電纜壽命需要超過二十年至三十年的地方-雙絞線。雙絞線是現在世界上最火紅的物理性LAN介質.每一條電纜包含了四隊獨立絕緣的銅線。每一對絞合在一起，用最大能力減緩電磁波的干擾。電纜線有越多雙絞線包含在裡面，電磁波就越難發生。雙絞線有兩種類型:非屏蔽式和屏蔽式。

Shielded twsited pair cable is built very similarly to unshielded twsited pair，except that the pair of wires are also surrouded by a metallic shielded (like foil).This outer foil shielded minimize

the EMI to which the cable exposed.This increases the security and reliability of the cable .Unfortunately，however，this also increases the cost of the cable，so it is typecally used only in high-production envirements where the extra security is worth the extra cost.

遮蔽式雙絞線和非遮蔽式雙絞線的構造非常相似，那它們有甚麼地方不一樣呢?它們不同之處在於屏蔽雙絞線中成對的電線也被金屬屏蔽層(例如說金屬箔)包覆，外部箔屏蔽層可大幅減輕電纜因暴露在電磁波的干擾中所受的傷害，這個方法大幅增加了電纜的安全性和可靠度，但很不幸的是，這個方法會增加額外的成本，所以在需要多出來的成本產生額外的高安全性的那些高生產力環境比較常見。

The more common type of cabling is unshielded twsited pair cable.Instead of using extra shielded，this cable relies on the twsiting of its copper pairs to minimize the effects of EMI.Each twsited pair is twsited to insulate itself from the other pairs，and becuase there is no additional shielding，it's available at a lower cost than STP.Therefore，UTP is most common media of choice for small office,home office,residential and many commercial networks.

非遮蔽雙絞線類型比遮蔽式雙絞線類型的普及率還高，而且該類型的雙絞線不需要另外的遮蔽，它依靠銅線絞合來產生抵抗電磁波干擾的最大效能，每一個雙絞線經過絞合後將自身與其它絞線絕緣，再來因為它沒有額外的覆層，所以可以用比遮蔽行雙絞線低的價格取得它。因此非遮蔽型雙絞線對小型辦公室、家庭工廠、住宅和許多商業網路最常選擇的大眾媒體。

Twsited pair cable is categorized based on category (cat)number.For example,cat 3,category 3,is the oldest type of cable that we use.It was introducted back in the 1980s and 1990s for use in the first star topologies.The maximum throughput or bandwidth of cat 3 cablingis only 10 Mbps(megabits per second).The cat 3,like all twsited pair cabling,can't go ferther than 100 meters without using a repeater,switch,or active hub to amplify the signal.This limited distance is attributable to the copper wiring in these cables. As the signal travels over the copper wire,the netural resistance to wire decreases the signal's strength,and it eventually become too weak for the distant end to receive.For this reason,there is a 100 meter limitation when building and designing a twsited pair network.

雙絞線是以類別加數字來做分類。舉例來說，我們使用最早的電纜類型是類別3(cat 3)。這要從80年代和90年代用於第一批星型拓樸結構開始介紹起，cat 3電纜的最大吞吐量或頻寬只有10Mbps。cat 3電纜和其它雙絞線電纜一樣，使用中繼器、交換機或有源集線器來放大信號，cat 3才能超過100米。這個有限的距離歸因於在電纜中的銅線。當在銅線上傳輸信號時，銅線的自然電阻會減弱信號的強度，減弱到最後會無法接收遠端資訊。也因為如此，這100公尺的長度變成是建構或設計光纖網路時的阻礙。

Cat 5 cables were an improvement on the older cat 3 cables and contain more twists, giving them more shielding from EMI and a maximum bandwidth of 100 Mbps. Cat 5e, or cat 5 enhanced, increased the speed of the maximum throughput to one Gbps, or 1000 Mbps, and became a gap filler until cat 6 cabling became commonplace.

Cat 5電纜比Cat 3電纜更進一步擁有更多的雙絞線，這些雙絞線讓Cat 5電纜抵抗電磁波干擾時有更強的能力，而且擁有最大吞吐量來到100Mbps或1000Mbps的效能，並且填補在Cat 6電纜更普及化前的空缺。
